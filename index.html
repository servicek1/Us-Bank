Frequently Asked Questions

How long does fund recovery take?

The time frame varies depending on the type of issue. Simple cases may be resolved in 3-5 business days, while more complex cases involving fraud investigations can take up to 45 days. We'll keep you updated throughout the process.

What information do I need to provide?

To help resolve your issue quickly, please have your account number, details of the transaction or issue, dates, amounts, and any relevant documentation ready when you contact us.

Is there a fee for these services?

Most account support services are provided free of charge. However, certain specialized services may have associated fees, which we'll disclose before proceeding with any work.

How can I prevent future issues?

We recommend enabling two-factor authentication, regularly monitoring your account activity, keeping your contact information updated, and being cautious of phishing attempts.
